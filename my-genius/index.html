4 DAYS OF THE WORLD'S BEST MUSIC IN THE HEART OF CHICAGO




This music festival which takes place in chicago is usually regarded as the biggest music festival in the world with 400,000 yearly attendees. It is also called lolla for short. Lolla started as a moving touring event in 1991, before ending up permanently in chicago.The event was founded by Perry Farrell, who now has a stage named after him.
