Since 1973, at least 200 people that were sentenced to death have been exonerated. Think about how many weren't saved.

On September 24, 2024, Marcellus Williams was killed by the state of Missouri. He was believed to be innocent.
On October 12, 2017, Robert Pruett was killed by the state of Texas. He was believed to be innocent.
